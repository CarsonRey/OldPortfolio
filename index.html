Same site, different screen size! Web responsiveness is imporant because you don't know how your audience will be accessing your site. I don't know about you but when a site is off-putting and poorly organized, it's very easy to just close that tab and go elsewhere. Coding responsively (and responsibly!) is thinking about who the viewer is and what they're looking for. Why?
